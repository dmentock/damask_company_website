Virtual testing

From benchmark to component-relevant conditions

Damage is introduced progressively rather than all at once. Each workflow begins with a benchmark validation case where the answer is known, then moves toward realistic microstructures and loading paths — so that by the time a case approaches component-relevant conditions, its behavior is understood and trusted.

Because the same field-based outputs carry through, damage predictions stay linked to the microstructure and loading that produced them, and remain comparable to measurable behavior.
