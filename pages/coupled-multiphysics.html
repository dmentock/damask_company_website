Coupled degradation & fracture

When chemistry and mechanics drive failure together, they belong in the same model. DAMASK runs them as coupled problems, so degradation and fracture inform one interpretation instead of living in separate analyses.

Chemo-mechanical coupling

Mechanics and chemistry in one interpretation

Hydrogen-assisted fracture and related degradation problems can be investigated as coupled studies, where the evolving chemical field and the mechanical response are solved together. The result is a single, consistent picture of where and why a material degrades — from crack-growth kinetics in aluminium alloys to grain-level chemo-mechanics in battery cathodes.
